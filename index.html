Copa Winners

2011 Winner : Uruguay

Uruguay won the tournament after defeating Paraguay 3–0 in the final, giving them a record 15th Copa América title and their first since 1995.

2015 Winner : Chile

The 2015 Copa América was the 44th edition of the Copa América, the main international football tournament for national teams in South America, and took place in Chile between 11 June and 4 July 2015.

2019 Winner : Brazil

Reduced to 10 men, Brazil beat Peru 3-1 in 2019 Copa America final to earn major trophy. South American football giants Brazil won the 2019 Copa America, beating Peru 3-1 on Sunday in the final.
